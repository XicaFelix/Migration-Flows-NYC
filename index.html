Population Shifts in New York City

What's This Project About?

New York City is a diverse city, with a unique culture. However, the population of NYC and other metropolitan cities has decreased during the pandemic. This project lets you see where everyone is going and coming from. The tableau at the top is Census data regarding how the population in the US has changed between 2020 and 2021. Feel free to enter a county name, and see where everyone has moved from.
